Types of Bento

There are many types of Bento, and they are also made in different places:

  • Makunouchi Bento is for formal meals
  • Kouraku Bento is for picnics
  • Ekiben is for Travellers
  • Kyaraben is art bento or cute bento
  • Hokaben is a warm Bento
  • Plain Bento is just a normal lunch
